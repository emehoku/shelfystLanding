The Problem

Collectors are scattered across platforms that were never built for the hobby. We deserve a better place.

Premium collectibles are expensive, emotional, and hard to replace — yet collectors still jump between social apps, marketplaces, and random DMs because nothing is truly built around the hobby itself.

Trust

High-value collectibles need a platform that takes trust seriously.

Before launch, we’re building a founding community and setting listing standards so the marketplace feels safer than random DMs.

Trust signals we’re designing for

Verified seller signals and transaction history (earned over time)
Listing quality standards: clear photos, condition, edition/ES, box/shipper, and shipping terms
Reputation that matches collector reality (not generic marketplace ratings)
Clearer deal flow: messages, holds, and status updates without chaos
Is this only for statues?

The platform is focused on premium figures, statues, busts, and high-end collectibles — including 1:6, 1:4, 1:3, life-size, resin statues, Hot Toys, Prime 1, Sideshow, Queen Studios, XM Studios, and similar collectibles. This is not a marketplace for cards, diecasts, Funko Pops, or unrelated collectible categories outside of the statue/figure community.

When is the initial release?

We’re currently building the initial release and onboarding founding collectors. Early access invitations will be sent to founding collectors first.

Will you spam my email?

No. We’ll only send important updates about the launch, beta access, platform progress, and major collector features.

Will there be seller verification?

Yes. Vault access will require additional verification steps such as email, phone, and ID verification to help create a safer collector Vault.

How is this different from Facebook groups?

This platform is built specifically for collectors — with advanced marketplace filters, collector profiles, reputation systems, AI-powered tools, grail tracking, and a dedicated collector feed.

Will there be buyer and seller ratings?

Yes. Verified profiles, transaction history, reputation signals, and community feedback will help build trust between collectors.

Will the platform support local pickup and trades?

Yes. Local pickup, trades, and collector-to-collector transactions are part of the vision for the marketplace.

Can I showcase my collection even if I’m not selling?

Absolutely. The platform is not just a marketplace — it’s also a social space to share setups, display rooms, grails, and collection progress.

Will there be fees for selling?

Details are still being finalized, but the goal is to create a fair and collector-friendly marketplace experience.

Can I become a founding collector?

Yes. Early members who join before launch may receive founding collector badges, priority early access, and exclusive community perks.
