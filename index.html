FAQ
Is this only for statues?

The platform is focused on premium figures, statues, busts, and high-end collectibles — including 1:6, 1:4, 1:3, life-size, resin statues, Hot Toys, Prime 1, Sideshow, Queen Studios, XM Studios, and similar collectibles. This is not a marketplace for cards, diecasts, Funko Pops, or unrelated collectible categories outside of the statue/figure community.

When is the initial release?

We’re currently building the initial release and onboarding founding collectors. Invitations will be sent to founding collectors first.

Will you spam my email?

No. We’ll only send important updates about the launch, beta access, platform progress, and major collector features.

Will there be seller verification?

Yes. Vault access is being designed around additional verification steps such as email, phone, and ID verification so serious sellers can build trust before high-value transactions.

How is this different from Facebook groups?

Facebook groups are temporary, fragmented, and hard to search. Shelfyst is built around permanent collector profiles, structured listings, reputation, transaction history, discovery, and market context.

Will there be buyer and seller ratings?

Yes. The goal is not influencer popularity or generic star ratings. Reputation should reflect collector reliability: completed transactions, communication, packaging quality, feedback, and dispute history.

What does transaction history mean?

It means completed sales, purchases, trades, and feedback can become part of a collector’s trust record. Long term, anonymized sold-history signals can also help collectors understand real market value.

Will the platform support local pickup and trades?

Yes. Local pickup, trades, and collector-to-collector transactions are part of the vision for the marketplace.

Can I showcase my collection even if I’m not selling?

Absolutely. The platform is not just a marketplace — it’s also a social space to share setups, display rooms, grails, and collection progress.

Will there be fees for selling?

Details are still being finalized, but the goal is to create a fair and collector-friendly marketplace experience.

How does founding access work?

Shelfyst opens in waves. Founding Collectors are invited in batches so we can keep quality high, protect trust standards, and improve the product with real collector feedback. Join the list now — we’ll email you when your wave opens.

Why not open to everyone at once?

Collectibles need trust — reputation, verification, and clear listings. Growing in waves lets us onboard serious collectors first and avoid the mess that happens when platforms scale too fast.

Can I become a founding collector?

Yes. Join the founding list before launch. Early members can receive a permanent Founding Collector badge, priority in early access waves, and input on trust and listing standards.
